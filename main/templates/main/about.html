About Us

Trusted & Faster Logistic Service Provider

Deep Logistics traces its roots to the respected legacy of Mr. Deepak Thakre, who has been serving the Maharashtra Government in the construction sector for over 35 years. From this strong foundation of trust, experience, and quality, the company expanded into logistics operations.

For the past three years, logistics has been a crucial part of our parent company's operations. With consistent growth, increasing demand, and a larger client footprint, we have now launched Deep Logistics as an independent, professionally managed logistics organisation.
